Our Wedding Packs

Designed to adapt to different needs and dreams, always with a personal touch. Base prices are: Essential Pack from €2000, Premium Pack from €3000, and Exclusive Pack from €4500.

Essential Pack

From €2,000

This basic package is designed for couples seeking assistance in key aspects of their wedding planning, while maintaining control over certain details.

Services Included:

  • Initial consultation: Meeting to define the wedding style and theme, establishing a personalized action plan.
  • Vendor selection: Recommendation of trusted professionals in areas such as photography, music, and catering.
  • Wedding day coordination: Supervision and time management to ensure everything unfolds as planned.

Premium Pack

From €3,000

Designed for couples who want more detailed and worry-free planning, this package offers comprehensive support at every stage of the process.

Essential Pack Services PLUS:

  • Full vendor management: Sourcing, selection, and communication with the best professionals to ensure quality and harmony.

  • Assistance in choosing attire: Advice and support in selecting outfits and accessories.
